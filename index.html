About

Offie Somutochukwu Chiagozie is a fun loving man, passionate and committed to all my endeavors. I am the fourth child of a family of seven children, five boys and two girls. My dad is a retired civil servant, and my mum a teacher. My family is my foundation, where i learned most of the good i know, honesty, hardwork and commitment to purpose etc. I owe my parents a lot for providing enabling environment for me thus I can't repay all they have sacrificed for me. I pray to be bouyant enough to be able to provide for them, while they are still alive.

As a child, i enjoyed reading and learning, which helped me in school, I wasn't the brightest in class but i didn't perform badly in my subjects as well. Skipping some of my elementary classes helped me graduate elemantary school at a very young age and also aided graduating secondary school before clocking eighteen years.

My tertiary academy was relatively fast, thou stalled by University strikes and students riots etc. It was still joyful and a pleasant memory to behold. I served in Nasarawa State, during my one year NYSC. Where i first learnt and witnessed a culture different from mine, for the first time. It was a pleasure being with people from different tribes, culture and background. It was noteworthy because all my formal education was in South Eastern part of Nigeria. NYSC exposed other parts of Nigeria to me beyond the one taught in class and few minority muslims/northerners i see in the East.

I have been privileged to work with some firms after my graduation, which helped me learn a few things about etiquettes of civil service/work place. I am currently in my thirties, still learning and unlearning and being prayful for better days.
